ABOUT

Tabby cats are known for their distinctive coat patterns, which include stripes, dots, lines, or swirling patterns, usually with an "M" shape on their foreheads. They are not a specific breed but rather a coat type seen in many cat breeds. Tabbies are often friendly, affectionate, and make great pets.
